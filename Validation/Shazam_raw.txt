'''Shazam''' may refer to:
{{tocright}}
==In the ''Captain Marvel'' comic book franchise==
* Shazam, formerly [[Captain Marvel (DC Comics)|Captain Marvel]], a Fawcett Comics and, later, DC Comics superhero
* [[Shazam (wizard)]], the wizard who gives Captain Marvel and the Marvel Family their powers
* [[Shazam! (film)|''Shazam!'' (film)]], a 2019 American superhero film
* [[Shazam! (TV series)|''Shazam!'' (TV series)]], a 1970s live-action television series based upon Captain Marvel's adventures
* ''Shazam!'', a comic book series about Captain Marvel published by [[DC Comics]] from 1973 to 1978
* ''[[Monster_Society_of_Evil#Shazam.21:_The_Monster_Society_of_Evil_.282007.29|Shazam! The Monster Society of Evil]]'', a 2007 limited edition DC Comics series written by Jeff Smith
* ''[[The Kid Super Power Hour with Shazam!]]'', a 1980s Saturday morning cartoon about Captain Marvel and  Family

==Other uses in arts and media==
* [[Foxy Shazam]], a rock band
* [[Shazam (album)|''Shazam'' (album)]], a 1970 LP by The Move
* "Shazam!", original title for Attack Attack!'s second album ''[[Attack_Attack!_(album)|Attack Attack!]]''
* Shazam! a New Zealand youth music programme from the 1980s, initially presented by [[Phillip Schofield]]
* [[Shazam! (song)|"Shazam!" (song)]], a 1960 song by Duane Eddy
* "Shazam!", a song by the Beastie Boys from their 2004 album ''[[To the 5 Boroughs]]''
* "Shazam!", a song by Spiderbait from their 1999 album ''[[Grand Slam (Spiderbait album)|Grand Slam]]''
* A catch-phrase used by the character [[Gomer Pyle]] in ''The Andy Griffith Show'' and ''Gomer Pyle, U.S.M.C.''
* ''Shazaam'', the [[False_memory#Commonly_held_false_memories|false memory]] of a 1990s movie that never existed

* [[SHAZAM (software)]], software package for econometrics and statistics
